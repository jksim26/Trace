# Decision Court

Three Qwen roles — Proposer, Guardian, Judge — deliberate a rule-pack-gated
conflict, write the reasoning a personally-liable QP can stand behind, and
persist every verdict to the court record. Scene 2 of the [[CLI Demo]] (the
REJECT verdict; the rejected proposal is preserved, never deleted).

# Bi-temporal time-travel

`get_valid_asof(date)` reconstructs what was decided, and known, as of any
past date — not just what's true now, but what a reasonable person on that
date would have believed, given what had (and hadn't yet) been superseded.
Scene 4 of the [[CLI Demo]] demonstrates this directly.

# SYNTHESIS

## verdict
Singapore has no golden-thread equivalent; this is an absence finding, so claim no SG counterpart exists, not that SG rejected one. The strong hook is the Qualified Person regime under Building Control Act section 9: a personal criminal due-diligence duty on a named architect or engineer for design and supervision, with three liability tracks. The only record analogue, reg 22(e) structural deviations, has no falsification detection. Pitch the demand, not a mandate.

## recommendedFraming
Home plus precedent plus scale; do not lead UK-only with a China and Alibaba panel. Singapore is the home market and demo: a named QP is criminally accountable but holds only fragmented structural paper. China is scale via its lifelong quality responsibility law. The UK golden thread is one line of global precedent. Tie to CORENET X and the IDD 70 percent by 2025 target.

## localizeDemo
Localize the demo to a 95m Singapore condo and keep the UK as a one-line close. Capture is non-combustible cladding per SCDF Fire Code Cl 3.5.1 over 15m. Invalidation is a swap to a polyethylene-core ACP banned over 15m; cite the Toh Guan Road fire of 2017, which would fail PFI and expose the QP to section 9 criminal liability. Use 15m not 18m, 40 buildings not 36, and note Toh Guan preceded Grenfell.
